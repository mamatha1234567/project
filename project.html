PROJECT TITLE:ESTIMATION OF DROUGHT PREDICTION USING DEEP LEARNING MODELS

ROLE                    :Team Lead

TECHNOLOGY  : deep learning models

DESCRIPTION   :

we predict the drought by using deep learning models like cnn,vgg16.This will helpful to agriculture. Agricultural is the primary source of income for the majority of the population.In recent decades, it has been discovered that soil fertility is linked to sustainable agriculture, and that preserving soil fertility can increase present crop output levels.CNN model has been applied for classification of real time image data obtained from satellite resources. Challenges still exist in drought prediction at long lead time and under a changing environment resulting from natural and anthropogenic factors. Hence the DL models like CNN and VGG16 transfer models are implemented and compared for their performances.
